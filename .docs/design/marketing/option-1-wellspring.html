02 / THE AGENTS

Three agents. One mandate.

A team that works the pipeline around the clock — and reports to you in plain language.

Sourcing agent

Searches · Scores · Enriches · Enrolls

Sweeps your sources for passive candidates, scores fit against the role, enriches every contact, and enrolls the shortlist into the loop.

Outreach agent

Drafts · Personalizes · Sequences

Writes each personalized touchpoint across email and LinkedIn — built on the candidate's real work, queued for your approval.

Main agent

Orchestrates · Answers · Supervises

Coordinates the others, keeps the loop moving, and answers you in chat — your single calm point of contact for the whole search.

03 / THE AUTONOMY DIAL

You decide how much it does on its own.

Hands on the wheel

You drive. The agent waits for instructions — sourcing, drafting, and sending happen only when you ask. Nothing moves on its own.

The default

The agent sources and drafts on its own, then queues every message — you approve each send. Momentum, with your hand on every outbound.

Set the guardrails, let it run

The agent sends within your caps — governed daily limits, full audit log, replies routed to your Inbox. Supervision by exception.

04 / STAY IN CONTROL

Autonomy you can account for.

Speed is only useful if you can trust it. Every send is governed, logged, and yours to approve.

Approve before it sends

Every outbound message can wait in an approval queue. Read it, edit it, release it — or hold it. The agent never goes around you.

Governed send caps

Set daily limits per seat and per channel. Even on Autopilot, the agent throttles to your caps — your domain reputation stays intact.

Full audit log

Who sourced whom, what was sent, when, and by which agent. Every action is recorded — for your team, your client, and compliance.
